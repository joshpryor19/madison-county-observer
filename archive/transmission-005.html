Five Years Under Observation

A reflection on five years of reporting from the quiet corners of Madison County.

Video transmission — coming soon

Five years is a long time to watch a small county go about its business.

It is long enough to see the patterns. Long enough to earn trust. Long enough to also collect a certain type of file.

Not the kind that makes headlines. The kind that explains them.

Thank you for reading, for writing, for calling, for trusting us with your stories. We do not take that lightly.

The Observer will continue doing what it has always done — watching, listening, asking questions, and reporting the truth as best we can find it.

Here’s to the next five.
